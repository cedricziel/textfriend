# TextFriend Privacy Policy

TextFriend does not collect any data. Period.

- No analytics, no tracking, no advertising identifiers.
- No accounts. No network connections are made by the app.
- Your documents are read and written only where you store them (on your
  device or in your own cloud storage via the Files app). They never pass
  through our servers — we don't have any.
- Editor preferences (font size, theme) are stored locally on your device.

If you have questions, open an issue at
https://github.com/cedricziel/textfriend/issues.
